R I Z A L

Explore the raw beauty and hidden wonders of our hometown, Rizal, which is located in the province of Kalinga. The concrete roads lead you to vast areas of agricultural lands from Liwan to San Quintin.

Even if Rizalians originate from different places, the people are friendly and ready to welcome you. If you want to go to Rizal, there are various places, food and events that you don't want to miss.
